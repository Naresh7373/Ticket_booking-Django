How to Book Train Tickets on BookingTrain?

Booking a train ticket online on the BookingTrain app or website is very simple. Follow the stepwise process for bus booking on BookingBus.
  • Enter Travel Details: Enter your source, destination & travel date to check the top-rated bus services available.
  • Search Train: Filter bus type, duration, bus timings, boarding and dropping point, number of seats available, user ratings & amenities for your desired route.
  • Choose Train: Select your preferred seat, boarding and dropping points. Once selected, check the ticket price details and proceed to pay.
  • Enter Passenger Details: Fill in the passenger information and contact details.
  • Payment: Once you fill in the information, click on “Proceed” to pay to complete the payment process.
  • Ticket Confirmation: After the payment process, receive the bus ticket confirmation on your registered email ID or mobile number.
